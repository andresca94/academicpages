A Case Study with Barbara Feldon from Get Smart Using ComfyUI

In this creative project, we explore various capabilities of ComfyUI through the iconic actress Barbara Feldon, best known for her role as Agent 99 in the classic 1965–1970 TV series Get Smart. We utilize advanced tools such as FLUX, Hunyuan3D, and AnimateDiff to process, enhance, and animate Barbara’s visuals. This case study demonstrates a complete AI-assisted creative workflow from image restoration to video generation.

Part One: Image Processing

Original Photographs of Barbara Feldon

Here we display original photos of Barbara Feldon, which serve as the basis for subsequent transformations and enhancements.

FLUX and Its Capabilities (2023)

FLUX is a powerful modular toolkit released in 2023 for ComfyUI that enables advanced inpainting, upscaling, animation, and LoRA training. It has revolutionized the AI art pipeline by streamlining creative workflows.

  1. Inpainting and Outpainting with FLUX + IC-Light (2023)
    Source: Internal ComfyUI module for scene relighting and enhancement. Allows advanced background editing and illumination control.
  2. FLUX Upscaler (2023)
    Enhances image resolution and quality to support better LoRA training and aesthetic fidelity.
  3. FLUX LoRA Character Consistency Training
    Using the enhanced photos to train a dedicated LoRA for consistent facial identity recreation. Built upon Stable Diffusion 1.5/2.1 architecture.
  4. 3D Front View with Hunyuan3D-2 (2024)
    Based on Trellis | Image-to-3D pipeline by Tencent (2024)
    Converts images to detailed 3D meshes using diffusion-based geometry generation.
  5. FLUX Depth Canny + ToonCrafter (2023)
    Extracts depth and edge data to enable cartoon-style interpolation and animation frames.
  6. FLUX Redux (2023)
    Official FLUX restyling toolkit to create variations in look and aesthetics using a LoRA-based approach.
  7. CatVTON (2023)
    High-fidelity virtual try-on system allowing realistic clothing overlays. Based on diffusion + NeRF fusion.

Part Two: Video Generation & Animation

In this phase, we apply generative and animation tools to bring Barbara Feldon to life through video sequences, dance animations, and stylized performances. These tools leverage the previously trained LoRA and processed visuals.

  • ReActor (2023) | Face Swap Module
    Face swapping using generated or original reference portraits.
  • Sonic (2023) | Lip-Sync Portrait Animation
    Generates accurate lip movements from audio input and portrait stills.
  • LivePortrait (2023) | Vid2Vid Module
    Uses a reference video to animate facial expressions and movements.
  • Hunyuan IP2V (2024) | Image-to-Video Engine
    Converts static images into dynamic scenes based on prompts and style guides.
  • IC-Light Video Relighting + AnimateDiff (2023)
    Combines lighting correction and animated frame generation for cinematic movement.
    Source: AnimateDiff paper (2023): "Animating with Diffusion Models" - arXiv:2307.04725
  • Hunyuan LoRA (2024)
    Applies comic-book or illustrative LoRA styles to animation frames.
  • CogvideoX Fun (2023) | Video-to-Video Cinematic Edit
    Allows stylized scene transformation from one video to another using deep frame matching.
  • Epic CineFX (2023)
    Enhances videos with cinematic color grading, transitions, and filters.
  • Hunyuan Video (2024)
    Advanced Video-to-Video adaptation guided by prompts and learned LoRA styles.
